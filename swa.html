Introduction

Technology, broadly defined, is the application of scientific knowledge to practical purposes, aiming to solve problems and improve human life. It encompasses a wide range of tools, techniques, and systems, from simple inventions like the wheel to complex innovations like the internet. Technology is a dynamic field, constantly evolving as new discoveries and innovations emerge

Technology offers numerous advantages, including increased efficiency and productivity, improved communication, easier access to information and educational resources, and enhanced collaboration. It also fosters innovation, opens new business avenues, and prepares individuals and businesses for the future.
